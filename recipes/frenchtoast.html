My French Toast Recipe

Description

This is a self explanatory meal if you dont know what it is then you need Jesus!! lol

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread (Choose bread of you liking )
  • Three Eggs
  • Half cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon cinamon
  • Vanilla
  • Nutmet
  • Berries (strawberries and blueberries)
  • Banana
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Wash and slice your berries
  2. In a bowl mix the eggs, milk, cinamon, nutmeg.
  3. Whisk the mixture
  4. Heat your pan and add butter on it
  5. Dip you bread slices into the mixture making sure that both sides of the bread is covered with the egg mixture
  6. put the soaked bread slices onto the hot buttered pan and let both sides cook to your liking
  7. well your french toast is ready put them on a plate
  8. Top the toasts with sliced berries and banana and syrup if you are into that
  9. Enjoy your handy work you deserve it
